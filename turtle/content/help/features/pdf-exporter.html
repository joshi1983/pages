The PDF exporter is available for some drawings after clicking Drawing -> Download.

The PDF exporter lets you download files that express how the drawing should be printed to paper.

Limitations

Not all drawings can be exported to PDF

Some features don't export well so the option is hidden.

  • Gradients don't export so drawings using them can't be exported to PDF
  • Alphacolors/semi-transparent fill colors can't be exported to PDF
  • 3D shapes such as spheres, cyllinders, cones aren't represented in PDF so drawings containing them are not exported to PDF.
  • Both circular and elliptical arcs can't be exported to PDF.
  • Rotated ellipses can't be exported to PDF.
  • Paths drawn or filled with miter join style and miter limits other than 10 can't be exported to PDF.

Limited Fonts

Commands such as setFontFamily and label work with a wide variety of fonts supported by web browsers. Only 14 standard PDF fonts are supported for exporting. This means a label in a font like 'Arial' in the drawing may use 'Courier' in the exported PDF.
