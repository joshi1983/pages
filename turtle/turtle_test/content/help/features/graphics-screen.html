The graphics screen lets you see your drawing.

Displacing or translating the camera

The origin point or (0, 0, 0) is initially centred in the graphics screen but you can change this by pressing your mouse down in the graphics screen and dragging it around.

If the camera is displaced at all, a "Home" button will appear in the lower right corner of the graphics screen. Click that to return to origin.

Zoom

You can zoom in and out of your drawing using Zoom -> In, Zoom -> Out from the menu.

You can also zoom in and out using your mouse wheel if you have a mouse with a mouse wheel. Hover your cursor over the graphics screen while turning your mouse wheel.

On a touch screen, you can also pinch 2 points or stretch it to zoom.

Coordinates Display

While moving the mouse over the graphics screen, the coordinates of your cursor show in the lower right corner.
